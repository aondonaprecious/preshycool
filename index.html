MY TECH JOURNEY

I just had a natural desire as a child, loved playing around computers. so I started to search and inquire about it,looking at the different fields in tech ,I decided to go with coding and it has not been an easy route. I studied sciences and for my university I searched for schools offering software engineering ,I found none, in government universities to be specific, beacuse i could not afford private. I took a step further going for computer science ,on reaching the school, we had to change to another course because it's was no longer available ,now am doing electrical electronics Engineering ,its been hard,tempted to take another field but I held on .am so grateful, I get to do what I love .
